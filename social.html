Collaborative Telepresence in Social Media

Participation in social events such online has increased with the telepresence technologies used to enable people to stream on social media platforms
These platforms include:

  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Snapchat
With each platforms supporting the ability to have what is called stories/status so people can watch what the person is doing at that point in time.
People can use these platforms to do live streaming with there followers and Instagram supports the ability to join into the video chat.


Collaborative Telepresence is impactful in many areas namely:

  • Media/Entertainment
  • Social Media
  • Education
  • Healthcare
