Collaborative Telepresence in Education

According to Campus Technology, “telepresence instruction is not about online learning at all. Instead, the goal is to extend the face-to-face experience of in-class instruction to remote areas and other schools.”


Students dealing with difficult situations are successfully able to attend and participate in their classes remotely via the telepresence robots instead of missing class sessions or dropping classes entirely


Collaborative Telepresence is impactful in many areas namely:

  • Media/Entertainment
  • Social Media
  • Education
  • Healthcare
