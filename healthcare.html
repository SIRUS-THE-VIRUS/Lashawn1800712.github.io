Collaborative Telepresence in Healthcare

A robot by the name of RITA (Reliable Interactive Table Assistant) by RoboValley out of the Netherlands.
This is more of a caregiver robot than telepresence, but because of its primary function, can be classified as telepresence.
RITA can monitor the behavior of a patient, alerting medical staff of significant changes, and allowing a care taker or physician to check in when alerts arise.


Video can be an effective way for healthcare organizations to provide care to patients; it enables efficient face-to-face collaboration among remote patients and specialists i.e telepresence.
Hospitals and clinics are embracing video for collaborative care for both the benefits to the patient and the bottom line


Collaborative Telepresence is impactful in many areas namely:

  • Media/Entertainment
  • Social Media
  • Education
  • Healthcare
