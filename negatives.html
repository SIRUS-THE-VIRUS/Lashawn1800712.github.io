Negative Conotations of Collaborative Telepresence

The use of virtual reality technology, especially for remote control of machinery or for apparent participation in distant events, has a number of negative connotations.

  • Lack of Face-To-Face Interaction

  • Possibility of Network Failure and Equipment Breakdown

  • Language and Cultural Differences

  • Incidences of Group Think

Just because there are some negative things associated with collaborative telepresence does not mean people should completely avoid the use of such technologies

Collaborative Telepresence is impactful in many areas namely:

  • Media/Entertainment
  • Social Media
  • Education
  • Healthcare
