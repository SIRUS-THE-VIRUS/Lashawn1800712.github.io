Collaborative Telepresence in Media/Entertainment

No longer do we need to be physically at a location to find entertainment.
With the introduction of live streaming platforms such as Twitch.tv,Mixer and Youtube, we can sit at home and enjoy pre-recorded events or a
live stream of anything from sporting events to video games and competitions.
Live streaming is the broadcasting of real-time, live video to an audience over the internet.
All you need to be able to live stream is an internet-enabled device, like a smart phone or tablet, and a platform to broadcast on.


Collaborative Telepresence is impactful in many areas namely:

  • Media/Entertainment
  • Social Media
  • Education
  • Healthcare
